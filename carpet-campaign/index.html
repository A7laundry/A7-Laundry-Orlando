Orlando Carpet Facts

What Every Orlando Homeowner
Should Know About Carpets

4x

Carpets can trap up to 4 times their weight in dirt, dust, allergens, and bacteria, according to the EPA.

74%

Orlando's average relative humidity is 74%, creating ideal conditions for mold growth in unclean carpet fibers. Regular professional cleaning is essential.

97%

Professional hot water extraction removes up to 97% of dirt and bacteria, according to the IICRC (Institute of Inspection, Cleaning and Restoration Certification).

6-12mo

Carpet manufacturers recommend professional cleaning every 6-12 months. Homes with pets or children in Orlando's climate should clean every 3-6 months.
