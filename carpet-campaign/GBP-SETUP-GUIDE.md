## 7. REVIEW STRATEGY

### How to Ask
1. **Text/SMS** within 1-2 hours of job completion (highest conversion)
2. **Email** same-day follow-up
3. **In-person** — technician asks at end of job
4. **QR code** on invoice/receipt
5. **Gentle reminder** 3-5 days later if no review

### Review Request Template (SMS)
```
Hi [Name]! Thank you for choosing A7 Service Premium for your carpet cleaning today. We hope you love the results! Would you mind leaving us a quick Google review? It helps our small business a lot.

[Your Google Review Link]

Thank you! — The A7 Team
```

### Target
- **2-4 new reviews per week** (steady, not in bursts)
- Encourage customers to mention specific services and neighborhoods

### Response Templates

**Positive Review:**
```
Thank you so much, [Name]! We're thrilled that our carpet cleaning team could [reference specific detail from their review] in your [neighborhood] home. It was a pleasure working with you, and we look forward to your next cleaning! — The A7 Service Premium Team
```

**Negative Review:**
```
Hi [Name], we sincerely apologize for this experience. This does not reflect our usual standard of service. We'd like to make this right — please call us directly at (407) 555-0197 so we can discuss a solution. Thank you for bringing this to our attention.
```

### Rules
- Respond to ALL reviews within 24 hours
- NEVER offer incentives for reviews
- NEVER selectively ask only happy customers
- Include a keyword naturally in responses
