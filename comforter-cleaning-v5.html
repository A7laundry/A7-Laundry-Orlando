The invisible reality

Even a clean-looking comforter carries more than you think.

Dust Mites & Allergens

Comforters are among the most common sources of household allergens. Over time, dust mites accumulate inside the fill and fabric — invisible to the eye, but present every night.

Regular Washing Falls Short

Home machines aren't built for oversized comforters. The fill compresses and never fully agitates, rinses or dries — leaving allergens and bacteria behind even after a full cycle.

Your Family Deserves Better

Children and pets spend a lot of time on the bed. A professionally cleaned comforter means a genuinely cleaner sleep environment for everyone in the home.
