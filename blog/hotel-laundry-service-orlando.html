Staying at a Hotel in Orlando? Your Laundry Options

Hotel guests in Orlando generally have three ways to get laundry done. Each has a place — but for most visitors, one is clearly easier.

Hotel in-house laundry — convenient but pricey and limited

Most hotels offer a laundry/valet service. It's convenient, but it's typically billed per garment — a few dollars per shirt, more for pants — so a normal week's pile can cost more than you'd expect. Turnaround often depends on the hotel's schedule, and it's built for pressing a shirt for a meeting, not washing and folding a family's vacation laundry.

Self-service laundromats — not ideal without a car

Orlando has laundromats, but they're rarely walking distance from the tourist hotels, and getting there without a car means a rideshare each way plus an hour or two feeding machines. If you'd rather not spend a vacation afternoon at a laundromat, see our honest laundromat vs delivery comparison.

Laundry pickup & delivery — the hands-off option

With laundry pickup and delivery in Orlando, a service collects your laundry from the hotel, washes and folds it by the pound, and delivers it back. You stay at the pool. No quarters, no car, no folding — and per-pound pricing is usually friendlier than per-item hotel rates.
