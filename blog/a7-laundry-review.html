Who Is A7 Laundry?

A7 Laundry is an Orlando-based concierge laundry service that specializes in vacation rental clients and tourists visiting the Disney-area resort communities. The company was founded specifically to solve the laundry problem that both vacation rental hosts and their guests face in Central Florida: professional-quality washing without the logistics of managing it yourself.

Unlike traditional laundromats or basic pickup services, A7 Laundry operates as a full-service partner for hosts — handling recurring schedules, high-volume linen loads, and back-to-back turnover situations that are routine in Orlando's short-term rental market.

  • Commercial-grade washing — industrial machines that outclean any home washer

Who It's Best For (and Who It's Not)

A7 Laundry is ideal for:

  • Vacation rental hosts in the Orlando/Kissimmee area managing any number of properties
  • Disney-area tourists staying a week or more without in-unit washer access
  • Families needing same-day turnaround after a pool-heavy day
  • Property managers handling multiple units on overlapping schedules
  • Hosts who want recurring service set up once and running automatically

A7 Laundry is not ideal for:

  • Someone who prefers to watch or supervise their laundry being done
  • Ultra-budget travelers who are comfortable spending hours at a coin laundromat
  • One-time, very small loads (less than 5 lbs) where per-pound pricing may not feel worthwhile
