Staying at an Airbnb in Orlando? Your Laundry Options as a Guest

As a guest, you've basically got three ways to handle laundry during your stay — and for most trips, one is clearly the least painful.

No washer — or a small, slow one

Many Orlando rentals either have no in-unit washer or a compact one that takes hours to get through a family's worth of clothes. Running it on vacation also means babysitting cycles instead of enjoying the trip.

The laundromat detour

There are laundromats around, but they're rarely close to the resort communities, and getting there without a car means a rideshare each way plus an hour or two feeding machines. If you're weighing it, see our honest laundromat vs delivery comparison.

Pickup & delivery — the hands-off option

With Orlando laundry pickup service, we collect your laundry from the rental, wash and fold it by the pound, and deliver it back. No quarters, no car, no folding — and per-pound pricing handles big piles easily. You stay at the pool.

A7 Laundry runs on WhatsApp — no app, no account. The whole process:

Perfect for Families, Long Stays & Group Trips
