Why Family Trips Generate So Much Laundry in Orlando

Orlando is a uniquely laundry-heavy vacation. A few reasons the pile grows so fast:

  • The heat — Florida humidity means sweaty park clothes, every single day.
  • The pool — wet swimsuits and pool towels pile up fast (and shouldn't sit damp).
  • The kids — younger ones often go through two or three outfits a day.
  • The length — a week-long trip easily outlasts what most families packed.

A family can generate 20–40 lbs of laundry across a stay — far more than a small rental washer or a per-item hotel service is built to handle.
