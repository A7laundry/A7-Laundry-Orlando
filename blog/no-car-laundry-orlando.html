Plenty of Orlando visitors skip the rental car — they use rideshare, resort shuttles, or park hoppers, and never really need to drive. But that makes one thing awkward: laundry. Dragging bags to a laundromat by rideshare is a hassle. Good news — you don't have to.

Doing Laundry in Orlando Without a Car

Without a car, your realistic options thin out fast. Here's the honest rundown:

In-unit or hotel guest laundry

If your rental has a washer, great — but many are small, and you'll spend your vacation babysitting cycles. Hotel guest laundry rooms exist but are often small, coin-operated, and busy.

Laundromat by rideshare

Possible, but it means a rideshare each way plus an hour or two of your day — not ideal, and it adds up. See our laundromat vs delivery comparison.

Pickup & delivery — the no-car winner

This is the one built for people without a car: we come to your door, take the laundry, and bring it back clean. No driving, no rideshare, no laundromat. It's Orlando laundry pickup service, and it's the same price whether you have a car or not.

Why Pickup Is Ideal When You're Car-Free

  • We come to you — hotel, resort, or Airbnb, free pickup.
  • No rideshare costs to and from a laundromat.
  • No lost vacation time sitting with the machines.
  • You don't need to be there — a front-desk or door handoff works.

Works From Hotels and Airbnbs
