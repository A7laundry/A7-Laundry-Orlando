The Problem

Your home washer
can't handle this.

A standard home drum can't agitate a King comforter properly. The fill compresses, detergent gets trapped, and incomplete drying causes mildew within 48 hours. You won't see it. Your guests will smell it.

  • ✕
    Unbalanced spin destroys fibers. Oversized comforters knot up and tear in residential washers.
  • ✕
    Detergent residue traps allergens. Without full rinse cycles, dust mites and dander cling to fill.
  • ✕
    Heat shrinks down fill. High-temp residential dryers clump natural down permanently.
  • ✕
    Damp drying = mildew in 24h. If moisture stays in the fill, mold spores grow inside the channels.
