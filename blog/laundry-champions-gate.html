The Laundry Challenge in Champions Gate

The laundry math in Champions Gate works against standard approaches:

  • Large homes, large loads: A 6-bedroom resort home generates 12+ sheet sets, 20+ towels, pool linens, and kitchen items per turnover
  • In-unit machines are undersized: Most vacation rental washers are residential units — one load at a time, 45-60 minutes per cycle. A full turnover takes 5-7 hours of sequential washing
  • Back-to-back turnovers leave no buffer: Saturday checkout / Saturday checkin gives a 3-4 hour cleaning window. There's no time to run laundry on-site
  • Property managers need scale: If you manage 5+ properties in Champions Gate, on-site washing for each becomes operationally impossible during peak weekends

The solution that Champions Gate hosts and property management companies use: dedicated laundry pickup service. Strip beds, bag linens, hand them off, and let the cleaners focus on what they do best.
