Best Laundry Pickup & Delivery in Orlando, FL (2026 Guide)

April 10, 2026 · 6 min read · By A7 Laundry

Orlando is one of the most visited cities in the United States — with millions of tourists flooding into vacation rentals, resorts, and hotels every year. That creates a massive demand for one thing most travelers don't think about until they're knee-deep in it: laundry.

Whether you're a vacationer who packed too light, an Airbnb host managing back-to-back guest turnovers, or a long-term resident who just doesn't want to spend your Saturday at a laundromat — this guide covers everything about laundry service in Orlando, FL.

What Types of Laundry Services Exist in Orlando?

Orlando has several options for getting your laundry done. Here's what you'll typically find:

  • Self-service laundromats — You drop off, wash, dry, and fold yourself. Cheap, but time-consuming.
  • Drop-off wash & fold — You bring your laundry, they wash and fold it, you pick it up. Usually $1.50–$2.50/lb.
  • Pickup & delivery laundry — They come to you, do the laundry, and bring it back. Most convenient option for tourists and rental hosts.
  • Dry cleaning — For delicate fabrics, suits, and special garments that need professional treatment.

For vacation rental guests and hosts in the Orlando area, pickup and delivery laundry service is almost always the best option — it saves hours of driving and waiting, and the per-pound cost difference is usually minimal.

How Much Does Laundry Service Cost in Orlando?

Pricing varies by service type and provider, but here's a realistic breakdown:

  • Self-service laundromat: $1.50–$4 per load (plus dryer, plus your time)
  • Drop-off wash & fold: $1.80–$2.80/lb · minimum 10–15 lbs usually required
  • Pickup & delivery laundry: $2.50–$3.50/lb · free pickup & delivery
  • Comforter cleaning: $33–$45 per piece depending on size

What to Look for in a Laundry Service

1. Turnaround Time

If you're a tourist with a tight schedule, turnaround time matters a lot. Ask upfront: can they do same-day or next-day? A7 Laundry offers Express 6-hour service for guests who need their clothes back the same day.

2. Transparent Pricing

Avoid services with vague pricing or hidden minimum fees. A good laundry service tells you exactly what you'll pay per pound — and whether there's a minimum order.

3. Pickup & Delivery Included

Free pickup and delivery is now standard for premium laundry services in Orlando. If a service charges extra for delivery, that's a red flag — or at least factor it into the total cost comparison.
